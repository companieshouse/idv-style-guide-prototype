Evidence for verifying your identity

Online

You'll need one of the following types of photo ID:

  • biometric passport from any country
  • UK photo driving licence (full or provisional)
  • UK biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • UK biometric residence card (BRC)
  • UK Frontier Worker permit (FWP)

In person at the Post Office

You'll need one type of photo ID, such as a:

  • passport
  • UK or EU photo driving licence (full or provisional)
  • UK biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • national identity photocard from the EU, Norway, Iceland and Liechtenstein
