Enforcement and penalties

What happens if you do not verify your identity in time

! Warning If you do not comply with identity verification requirements on time, you will be committing an offence and may have to pay a financial penalty or fine.

You will also be unable to:

  • make any filings for your company or entity
  • start a new company or entity
  • register as a Companies House authorised agent (also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider, or ACSP)

Directors

If you continue to act as a director after the deadline without verifying, you will be committing an offence. The company (or entity) and all directors may also be committing an offence.

People with significant control (PSCs)

If you continue to be a PSC after the deadline without verifying, you will be committing an offence.
