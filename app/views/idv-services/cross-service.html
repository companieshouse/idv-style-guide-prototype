https://set-up-a-company-idv-prototype-f317085f854c.herokuapp.com/

Password: setup-idv

Description

Directors and PSCs need to provide their Companies House personal code for each role they hold at each company they're involved in. This is so that we can connect their verified identity to the correct name on our records.

There are different ways of doing this depending on their role, and on when they were appointed.

CS01 (confirmation statement) – existing directors

AD01 (appoint a director) – new directors of existing companies

PSC01 (notification of a PSC) – new PSCs of existing companies

SCRS (register a company) – new directors and PSCs of new companies
