Password: psc_statement

Description

All PSCs need to provide their Companies House personal code. This is so that we can connect their verified identity to the PSC name on our records.

They need to do this even if they've already provided their code for other roles at the same company (for example, if they're also a director), or for roles they hold at other companies.

This service can be used by a PSC providing their own personal code, or by someone doing it on their behalf.
