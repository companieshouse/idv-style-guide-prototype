GOV.UK One Login

Always use the full name: GOV.UK One Login.

Use ‘GOV.UK One Login’ as a noun.

Avoid the word ‘account’ when referring to a user's GOV.UK One Login. This:

  • helps with account confusion because many services have accounts, such as ‘childcare account’ and ‘personal tax account’
  • allows us to label services with return journeys and account-like functionality as ‘your accounts’ in a user's GOV.UK One Login

Examples:

  • Create a GOV.UK One Login
  • Sign in to your GOV.UK One Login
  • You need a GOV.UK One Login to use this service
  • Use your GOV.UK One Login details to sign in to personal tax account
  • GOV.UK One Login is new

Never use:

  • One Login (without ‘GOV.UK’)
  • GOV.UK One Sign In/Sign On
  • GOV.UK One Log In/Log On
  • GOV.UK One Login account
  • One Login for government
  • single sign-on for government
  • GOV.UK OneLogin/OneLogin
